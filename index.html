Pablo Moreno is a happy young designer

Inventive in all ways, I was raised in Spain, moved to France for studying arts and I'm wainting to relocate in Rotterdam next september! I'm putting a lot of energy on making websites for people, for things or for fun.

Daily life

In my daily life in Amiens I'm finishing my double master in UX+UI between the ÉSAD and the UTC. I also do a student job welcoming people at the Maison de la Culture d'Amiens who's a big cultural place in the city. On the weekends I spend some time with two kids for an association who fights against educational inequalities.

Tools

Since my childhood I liked computers so I know HTML, CSS, Javascript, Kirby and Indexhibit CMS, Stripe, VSCodium, Figma, Blender, and the Abode suite... you can see all these things at my digital stuff. I recently made a video-tutorial showing the simplest way I found to put websites online and update them quickly, like I'm doing now.

People

I like to work with friends, and develop ideas together. These people can be from my school, like Sam or Léo, or far from me, like Tim or Angèle (for whom I'm making a portfolio/shop website).
